How to get the most out of this

  • →The Professor's Practice Questions are her actual lecture questions — closest thing to the real exam. Start there.
  • →Study by comparison pairs — schizoid vs avoidant, OCD vs OCPD, severe anxiety vs panic, acute stress disorder vs PTSD. That is where the hard questions live.
  • →For priority questions: safety beats teaching, de-escalation beats restriction, assessment beats intervention, and least restrictive always comes first.
  • →Questions and answer choices reshuffle every time — matching and Select All That Apply score partial credit that counts toward your grade.
